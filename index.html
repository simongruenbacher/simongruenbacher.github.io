Home

My name is Simon Grünbacher. I study computer science at Johannes Kepler University Linz, Austria. I am currently employed at the Institute for Systems Software at JKU, working on the development of JavaWiz. It's a visual Java Debugger primarily intended for first-semester computer science students. The Frontend is written in VueJS and TypeScript, the Backend in Kotlin.

I am also working at the Institute for Integrated Circuits at JKU. There, I am working on combinatorial optimization under the supervision of Richard Kueng.

I was also employed in the FWF Project Equations in Universal Algebra under the supervision of Erhard Aichinger at the Institute of Algebra at JKU. The aim of the project was to investigate the computational complexity of solving equations over finite structures such as groups and rings.
